## Launching Izzy

Izzy offers multiple ways to access the app:

- **Global hotkey**: Press `Option + Space` (customizable) anywhere on your Mac to show or hide Izzy
- **Menu bar**: Click the Izzy icon in your menu bar to open the app
- **Applications folder**: Launch Izzy like any other macOS application

## Searching for music

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the search tab">
    Click the magnifying glass icon or press `Cmd + F` to focus the search bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Type your search query">
    Enter the name of a song, album, artist, or playlist. Results appear in real-time as you type.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse results">
    Search results are organized by category:
    - **Songs**: Individual tracks ready to play
    - **Albums**: Full album collections
    - **Artists**: Artist profiles with their music
    - **Playlists**: Curated collections
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Play or add to queue">
    Click any result to play it immediately, or use the **+** button to add it to your queue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switching music services

Izzy supports three music streaming services: YouTube Music, Tidal, and JioSaavn.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open settings">
    Click the gear icon (⚙️) at the bottom of the app or press `Cmd + ,`.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select music source">
    Under the **Music Source** section at the top, choose your preferred service from the dropdown menu:
    - **YouTube Music**: Stream millions of songs
    - **Tidal**: Hi-Res lossless audio up to 24-bit/192kHz FLAC
    - **JioSaavn**: India's largest music streaming platform
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure quick switch mode">
    Choose how the provider switch button in the top-left corner works:
    - **Dropdown Menu**: Click to open a menu and select any provider directly
    - **Click to Cycle**: Click to cycle through providers one by one
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  When switching services, your search results and cache will be cleared automatically to ensure fresh content from the new service.
</Note>

## Creating and managing playlists

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to playlists">
    Click the **Playlists** tab in the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create a new playlist">
    Click the **Create Playlist** button and give your playlist a name.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add songs to your playlist">
    Browse search results, favorites, or your queue, then click the **+** button on any track to add it to a playlist.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manage your playlist">
    - **Play**: Click any song in the playlist to start playback
    - **Reorder**: Drag songs to rearrange them
    - **Remove**: Click the remove button to delete songs from the playlist
    - **Delete**: Delete the entire playlist from the playlist menu
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using AI search

Izzy's AI-powered search lets you find music using natural language descriptions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your Gemini API key">
    1. Go to **Settings** (⚙️)
    2. Scroll to the **AI Services** section
    3. Enter your Gemini API key in the text field
    4. Get a free API key from [Google AI Studio](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/get-started)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable AI search">
    Toggle **Show AI Search Tab** to on in the AI Services settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use AI search">
    Click the **AI Search** button (✨) and describe the music you want in natural language.
    
    **Examples**:
    - "upbeat workout music"
    - "relaxing jazz for study"
    - "sad songs for rainy days"
    - "energetic electronic dance music"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse AI recommendations">
    Gemini AI will understand your request and suggest songs that match your description based on mood, genre, and style.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Getting AI-powered recommendations

The "For You" section on the Home tab provides personalized music recommendations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up AI recommendations">
    Add your Gemini API key in Settings under the **AI Services** section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Build listening history">
    Play some songs to help the AI understand your music taste. The more you listen, the better the recommendations.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View recommendations">
    Go to the **Home** tab and look for the "For You" section with the AI badge (✨).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Refresh for new suggestions">
    Tap the **refresh button** to get completely new recommendations instantly. Each refresh:
    - Provides fresh songs you haven't heard before
    - Filters out already played tracks automatically
    - Uses parallel API calls for ultra-fast results (2-4 seconds)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The AI analyzes your recently played songs to suggest similar tracks based on genre, mood, and style. The more you use Izzy, the better the recommendations become.
</Tip>

## Managing your library

### Favorites

Save your favorite tracks for easy access:

- Click the **heart icon** on any song to add it to your favorites
- Access all favorites from the **Favorites** tab
- Remove songs by clicking the heart icon again

### Recently played

Izzy automatically tracks your listening history:

- View all recently played songs in the **Recently Played** tab
- Songs appear in reverse chronological order (newest first)
- Your history persists across app launches

### Queue management

Control what plays next with the queue:

- View the current queue from the playback controls
- Add songs to the queue using the **+** button
- Reorder songs by dragging them
- Remove songs from the queue
- Clear the entire queue with one click

## Downloading songs (Tidal only)

When using Tidal as your music source, you can download songs in high quality.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Switch to Tidal">
    Set your music source to **Tidal** in Settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find a song to download">
    Download buttons are available on:
    - Search results
    - Favorites
    - Queue
    - Playlists
    - Currently playing song
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click the download button">
    Click the download icon on any song. Izzy will:
    - Download in the highest quality available (Hi-Res Lossless > Hi-Res > Lossless > High)
    - Embed album artwork into the file using FFmpeg
    - Include full metadata (title, artist, album)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find your downloads">
    Downloaded songs are saved to `~/Downloads/Izzy Music/`. You'll receive a macOS notification when each download completes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  FFmpeg must be installed on your system for album art embedding. Without FFmpeg, songs will still download but won't include embedded artwork.
</Note>

## Playback controls

### Basic controls

- **Play/Pause**: Click the play button or press `Space`
- **Next track**: Click the next button or press `→`
- **Previous track**: Click the previous button or press `←`
- **Volume**: Click the volume icon and drag the slider, or use `↑` and `↓` keys
- **Seek**: Click anywhere on the progress bar or drag the playhead

### Mini player

Enable the mini player for a compact, floating interface:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Toggle **Mini Player** to on
3. A draggable, resizable window appears with full playback controls
4. Features liquid glass design and stays on top of other windows

### Menu bar player

Control music directly from your menu bar:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Toggle **Menu Bar Player** to on
3. Click the Izzy icon in your menu bar to access playback controls and album artwork

## Viewing lyrics

Izzy supports synchronized lyrics for songs:

1. Start playing a song
2. Click the **lyrics button** in the playback interface
3. Lyrics will display and scroll automatically with the music

<Note>
  Lyrics availability depends on the music service and whether the song has synchronized lyrics available.
</Note>

## Background playback

Izzy continues playing music in the background when you:

- Hide the app window with the global hotkey
- Switch to other applications
- Minimize the window

Playback will continue uninterrupted, and you can control it using:

- macOS media keys (play/pause, next, previous)
- Menu bar player
- Mini player window
- Global hotkey to show the main window
