Access settings by clicking the gear icon (⚙️) at the bottom of the app or by pressing `Cmd + ,`.

## Music source

Choose your preferred music streaming service.

| Setting | Options | Default |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **Music Source** | YouTube Music, Tidal, JioSaavn | YouTube Music |
| **Quick Switch Mode** | Dropdown Menu, Click to Cycle | Dropdown Menu |

**Music Source** determines which streaming service Izzy uses to search for and play music:

- **YouTube Music**: Stream millions of songs from YouTube's music catalog
- **Tidal**: Hi-Res lossless audio up to 24-bit/192kHz FLAC with quality badges
- **JioSaavn**: India's largest music streaming platform

**Quick Switch Mode** controls how the provider switch button (top-left corner) works:

- **Dropdown Menu**: Click to open a menu and select any provider directly
- **Click to Cycle**: Click to cycle through providers one by one

<Note>
  When you change music sources, Izzy automatically clears the search cache to ensure fresh results from the new service.
</Note>

## Interface settings

### Global hotkey

Customize the keyboard shortcut to show or hide Izzy.

| Setting | Options | Default |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **Hotkey Modifier** | Option, Control, Command, Shift + Option | Option |

**Available modifiers**:

- **Option (⌥)**: Recommended default, rarely conflicts with other apps
- **Control (⌃)**: Alternative modifier key
- **Command (⌘)**: May conflict with Spotlight (Command + Space)
- **Shift + Option (⇧⌥)**: Two-key combination for extra safety

<Warning>
  If you choose **Command**, you may need to change Spotlight's shortcuts in **System Settings** > **Keyboard** > **Keyboard Shortcuts** to avoid conflicts.
</Warning>

### Menu bar player

Show a compact music player in the macOS menu bar.

| Setting | Type | Default |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Menu Bar Player** | Toggle | On |

When enabled, a menu bar icon displays:

- Album artwork thumbnail
- Click to reveal playback controls
- Song information
- Quick access to show/hide main window

### Mini player

Show a floating, resizable mini player window.

| Setting | Type | Default |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Mini Player** | Toggle | Off |

**Features**:

- Draggable and resizable window
- Liquid glass design
- Always stays on top of other windows
- Full playback controls
- Album artwork display

### Liquid glass effect

Transform the app with a stunning liquid glass aesthetic.

| Setting | Type | Default |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Liquid Glass Effect** | Toggle | Off |

**What it does**:

- Full transparency throughout the app
- Enhanced dark mode
- Modern glassmorphism visual style
- Blur effects on backgrounds

### Your name

Personalize the home screen greeting.

| Setting | Type | Default |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Your Name** | Text field | "User" |

This name appears on the home screen welcome message.

### Window position

Control where the Izzy window appears on your screen.

| Setting | Action | Description |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| **Center Window** | Button | Centers the window on your screen |

**How it works**:

- Drag the handle at the top of the window to reposition it
- Izzy remembers the position between sessions
- Click **Center Window** to reset to the center of your screen

## AI services

Enable AI-powered search and recommendations with Google Gemini.

| Setting | Type | Default |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Show AI Search Tab** | Toggle | On |
| **Gemini API Key** | Secure text field | Empty |

**Gemini API Key** enables:

- **AI Search**: Find music using natural language ("upbeat workout music")
- **For You recommendations**: Personalized song suggestions based on listening history
- **Smart suggestions**: AI analyzes your taste to recommend new music

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get a free API key">
    Visit [Google AI Studio](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/get-started) to get your free Gemini API key.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add the key to Izzy">
    Paste your API key into the **Gemini API Key** field in Settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify it's saved">
    You'll see a green checkmark with "Key saved" when the key is successfully stored.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use the **Show Key** / **Hide Key** button to toggle visibility of your API key for verification.
</Tip>

## Playback settings

### Playback controls layout

Customize how playback controls are displayed.

| Setting | Type | Default |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Center Playback Buttons** | Toggle | Off |
| **Minimal Playback Player** | Toggle | Off |

**Center Playback Buttons**: When enabled, Previous, Play/Pause, and Next buttons are centered. When disabled, they're left-aligned.

**Minimal Playback Player**: When enabled, the playback player uses a more elegant and compact design with:

- Refined horizontal layout
- Subtle visual elements
- Integrated controls
- Cleaner appearance

## Updates

Manage how Izzy handles app updates.

| Setting | Type | Default |
|---------|------|--------|
| **Automatic Updates** | Toggle | On |
| **Check for Updates** | Button | - |

**Automatic Updates**: When enabled, Izzy automatically checks for and notifies you about new versions.

**Check for Updates**: Manually check for the latest version at any time.

<Info>
  Update checks may fail for development builds if the update server is not configured. This is normal for non-release versions.
</Info>

## Library stats

The Settings screen displays quick stats about your music library:

### Favorites

- Shows total count of favorited songs
- Displays thumbnails and titles of your most recent 3 favorites
- Indicates if you haven't favorited any songs yet

### Recently played

- Shows total count of recently played songs
- Displays thumbnails and titles of your most recent 3 plays
- Indicates if you haven't played any songs yet

## Debug options

<Warning>
  Debug options are intended for testing and development. Use with caution.
</Warning>

| Setting | Action | Purpose |
|---------|--------|--------|
| **Reset First Launch Flag** | Button | Resets the app to treat the next launch as a first-time launch |

This is useful for testing the startup tab functionality.

## Settings storage location

All settings are stored in your user library:

```
~/Library/Containers/com.superman.Izzy/Data/Library/Application Support/
```

Settings persist across app launches and updates.
