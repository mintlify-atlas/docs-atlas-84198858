## Launch the app

There are three ways to open Izzy Music Player:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Applications folder" icon="folder">
    Open Finder, go to Applications, and double-click Izzy
  </Card>
  <Card title="Spotlight search" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Press `Cmd + Space`, type "Izzy", and press Enter
  </Card>
  <Card title="Global hotkey" icon="keyboard">
    Press `Option + Space` from anywhere (after first launch)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  The global hotkey `Option + Space` is the fastest way to access Izzy. It works from any application and toggles the window open or closed.
</Tip>

## Choose your music service

Izzy supports three streaming platforms. Select your preferred service:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open settings">
    Click the Settings icon (⚙️) in the bottom corner of the Izzy window.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a service">
    At the top of the settings panel, choose from:
    - **YouTube Music** - Millions of songs from YouTube's catalog
    - **Tidal** - Hi-Res lossless audio (up to 24-bit/192kHz FLAC)
    - **JioSaavn** - India's largest music streaming platform
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start streaming">
    The service switches immediately. Close settings and start searching for music.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You can switch between services at any time. Your queue and favorites are preserved per service.
</Info>

## Search for music

Finding music in Izzy is fast and intuitive:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Focus the search bar">
    Click the search bar at the top of the window or press `Cmd + F` to focus it.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Type your query">
    Enter a song name, artist, album, or playlist. Search results appear in real-time as you type.
    
    Examples:
    - "Bohemian Rhapsody"
    - "Taylor Swift"
    - "Dark Side of the Moon"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse results">
    Results are organized by category: Songs, Artists, Albums, and Playlists. Scroll through to find what you want.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Play or queue">
    Click a song to play it immediately, or click the **+** button to add it to your queue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Use AI-powered search

For natural language queries, try AI search:

1. Click the **AI Search** button (✨) in the search bar
2. Describe the music you want in plain English:
   - "upbeat workout music"
   - "relaxing jazz for studying"
   - "90s hip hop classics"
3. Gemini AI will find songs matching your description

<Note>
  AI search requires a Gemini API key. Get one free from [Google AI Studio](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/get-started) and add it in Settings.
</Note>

## Play your first song

Once you've found a song, playing it is simple:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a song">
    Click on any song from your search results, a playlist, or the "For You" recommendations.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Playback begins">
    The song starts playing immediately. Album artwork appears in the player, and the track is added to your queue.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use playback controls">
    Control playback with the centered control buttons:
    - **Play/Pause** - Toggle playback
    - **Previous** - Go to the previous track
    - **Next** - Skip to the next track
    - **Seek bar** - Click or drag to jump to any position in the track
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Keyboard shortcuts for playback

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `Space` | Play/Pause |
| `→` | Next track |
| `←` | Previous track |
| `↑` / `↓` | Volume up/down |

<Tip>
  Media keys on your keyboard (play, pause, next, previous) work natively with Izzy.
</Tip>

## Manage your queue

The queue shows what's playing next and gives you full control:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the queue">
    Click the **Queue** tab in the sidebar to view all queued tracks.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add songs to queue">
    Click the **+** button on any song to add it to the end of your queue.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Reorder tracks">
    Drag and drop songs to rearrange the queue order.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Remove tracks">
    Click the **×** button on any song to remove it from the queue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Use the menu bar player

Izzy integrates beautifully with the macOS menu bar:

1. Look for the Izzy icon in your menu bar (top-right corner of your screen)
2. Click it to see the current track with album artwork
3. Control playback directly from the menu bar without opening the main window
4. Toggle menu bar player on/off in Settings if desired

<Info>
  The menu bar player shows the currently playing song and provides quick playback controls. It's perfect for checking what's playing without interrupting your workflow.
</Info>

## Access AI recommendations

Get personalized music suggestions based on your listening history:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Gemini API key">
    Go to Settings and add your Gemini API key. Get one free from [Google AI Studio](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/get-started).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Home tab">
    Click the **Home** tab in the sidebar to see the "For You" section with the AI badge (✨).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore recommendations">
    The AI analyzes your recently played songs and suggests similar tracks based on genre, mood, and style.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Refresh for new suggestions">
    Click the **refresh button** to get completely new recommendations. Each refresh provides fresh songs you haven't heard before.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Play a few songs first to build your listening history. The more you listen, the better the AI recommendations become.
</Tip>

## Save your favorites

Mark songs you love for quick access later:

1. Click the **heart icon** (♥) on any song to add it to your favorites
2. Navigate to **Library** > **Favorites** to see all your saved tracks
3. Click the heart again to remove a song from favorites

Favorites are saved per music service, so your YouTube Music favorites are separate from your Tidal favorites.

## Create a playlist

Organize your music into custom playlists:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to playlists">
    Go to **Library** > **Playlists** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create a new playlist">
    Click the **"Create Playlist"** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Name your playlist">
    Enter a name for your playlist and confirm.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add songs">
    Click the **+** button on any track throughout the app to add it to your playlists.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Download songs (Tidal only)

If you're using Tidal, you can download songs in Hi-Res quality:

1. Find a song you want to download (search results, favorites, queue, etc.)
2. Click the **download button** on the track
3. Songs are downloaded with embedded album art and metadata
4. Find downloaded files in `~/Downloads/Izzy Music/`
5. Receive a macOS notification when the download completes

<Note>
  Downloads prioritize the highest quality available: Hi-Res Lossless > Hi-Res > Lossless > High quality.
</Note>

## Master the global hotkey

The `Option + Space` hotkey is your fastest friend:

- **From any app**: Press `Option + Space` to instantly open Izzy
- **Izzy is open**: Press `Option + Space` to hide it
- **Customize it**: Change the hotkey in Settings > Keyboard

This floating window experience means Izzy is always just a keystroke away, perfect for controlling music while you work.
