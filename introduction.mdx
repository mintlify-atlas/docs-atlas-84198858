## Welcome to Izzy Music Player

Izzy is a battery-efficient macOS music player that brings together YouTube Music, Tidal, and JioSaavn in one beautiful, modern interface. Built with Swift and SwiftUI, Izzy delivers a seamless music experience with Hi-Res audio support, AI-powered recommendations, and an elegant glassmorphism design.

## Core capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Global hotkey access" icon="command">
    Press `Option + Space` anywhere on your Mac to instantly open or close Izzy. Perfect for quick music control without disrupting your workflow.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="AI-powered recommendations" icon="sparkles">
    Get personalized song suggestions using Gemini AI. The "For You" section analyzes your listening history and delivers fresh recommendations based on genre, mood, and style.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Menu bar integration" icon="window">
    Beautiful menu bar player with album artwork keeps your current track visible at all times. Control playback without opening the main window.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Hi-Res downloads" icon="download">
    Download songs in Hi-Res quality with embedded album art and metadata. Files are saved to `~/Downloads/Izzy Music/` with full metadata tagging.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Smart queue management" icon="list">
    Full control over your playback queue. Add, remove, reorder tracks, and create custom listening sessions.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Persistent playback state" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    Resume playback exactly where you left off. Your queue, position, and volume are preserved across sessions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Design philosophy

Izzy features a modern glassmorphism design with beautiful blur effects, smooth animations, and an elegant dark theme. The interface is optimized for various screen sizes and includes:

- **Floating window** - Always-on-top interface for quick music control
- **High-quality album artwork** - Stunning visuals for every track
- **Centered playback layout** - Perfectly balanced controls for an elegant experience
- **Responsive design** - Adapts beautifully to your workflow

<Info>
  Izzy requires **macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later** and **Python 3.13+** to run. Check the [installation guide](/installation) for detailed requirements.
</Info>
